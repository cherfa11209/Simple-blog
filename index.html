A christmas in the life of an aspiring Web Developer

Hello everyone, and happy holidays! It's been a very eventful years and a lot of us can say it's even been a horrible year. The Covid-19 Virus has changed our very way of being, most of us have decided to change the direction of our lives since then. I'm very grateful that I decided to learn to code this year, it's made me into a better person, I have really learned to love everything to do with computers. 3 practices that I have found to be very benefical are:

  • Daily practice
  • Keeping checklists
  • Setting small goals
  • Chatting with my peers on discord
  • Watching educational youtube videos
It's been such a pleasure that even on Christmas day all I've wanted to do is study and build my own websites. I would really like to thank The Odin Project, Andrei Neagoie and Harvard's CS50 for helping me through this journey, they have been wonderful resources, I would also like to give a special thanks to Stack Overflow for having all the answers to problems I couldn't have solved my self.
